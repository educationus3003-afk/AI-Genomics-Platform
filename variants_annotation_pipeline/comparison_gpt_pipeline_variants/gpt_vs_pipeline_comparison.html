Final verdict

All eight GPT-versus-pipeline pairs can now be compared. The uploaded ais.final.small_variants.annotated.vcf.gz has replaced the earlier tabix-index-only item.
The main disease-causing findings agree in all eight comparisons. For Fabry, Monilethrix, AIS, and NF1, the same central SNV and CNV are identified with consistent main gene, consequence, HGVS, ClinVar, AnnotSV, and ClassifyCNV interpretations where those fields apply.
The four complete SNV files are not record-for-record matches. Every pipeline SNV VCF contains 6 variants, whereas every GPT SNV VCF contains 19 variants. The 6 retained pipeline records are exact coordinate/allele matches and include the disease-causing variant, but 13 GPT background variants are not present in each pipeline output.

Overall conclusion: the biological conclusions match, all CNV coordinate sets match completely, and all disease-causing SNVs match. However, the full SNV record sets differ because the pipeline files retain only 6 of the 19 GPT records.
